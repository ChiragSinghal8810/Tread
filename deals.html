Requirements

  • Your company must be registered.
  • They are commercially active and have a sales history of at least 6 months.
  • You send the invoices to commercial customers ( B2B ) or public clients.
  • It is a service provided ( service or delivery of goods ).
  • You set at least one annual factoring volume in the amount of $ 500,000.00 around.
  • We only offer complete factoring. I.e. all customers with a payment term > 7 days are submitted.
  • Individual factoring compared to a single debitor is excluded.
